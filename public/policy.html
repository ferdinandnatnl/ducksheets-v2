Short Notice

Your bank statements are never stored on our servers. We do not have access to your data at any point during the conversion process, but we need to process your data to convert it into CSV format.

Bank statements can have varying formats, fonts, or even different spacing, which may impact how text in the PDF is extracted. We've put enough effort into handling them, but we cannot guarantee perfect accuracy for all PDFs, and advise you to review the generated CSV file to ensure that all information has been correctly extracted.

If you notice any inaccuracies, please feel free to contact us at tryducksheets@gmail.com or personally ferdinandnat@gmail.com.
